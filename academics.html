Courses

Lander's offers a wide range of courses designed to challenge and inspire students. From advanced computer science and finance to humanities and social sciences, our curriculum is comprehensive and dynamic. Our expert faculty provide a supportive and interactive learning environment, helping students excel in their academic and professional pursuits.

Student-Teacher Ratio

At Lander's, we pride ourselves on maintaining an exceptional 8:1 student-to-teacher ratio. This low ratio ensures that each student receives personalized attention and tailored instruction from our dedicated faculty. It fosters a more intimate and engaging learning environment, allowing for meaningful interactions and a deeper understanding of course material. Our commitment to small class sizes reflects our dedication to your academic success and personal growth.

Faculty

The faculty at Lander's are renowned for their expertise, dedication, and passion for teaching. Our professors are not only leaders in their respective fields but also deeply committed to the success and growth of their students. With a blend of academic excellence and real-world experience, they bring a wealth of knowledge and insight to the classroom. The close-knit, supportive environment at Lander’s allows faculty to provide personalized mentorship, fostering a collaborative and enriching educational experience for every student.

Majors

Lander's offers a diverse array of majors tailored to meet the interests and career aspirations of our students. From the innovative and rapidly-evolving field of Computer Science to the strategic and analytical world of Finance, our programs are designed to provide both theoretical knowledge and practical skills. We also offer majors in the humanities, social sciences, and STEM fields, ensuring a well-rounded educational experience. Each major is supported by experienced faculty who are dedicated to helping students achieve academic excellence and professional success.
